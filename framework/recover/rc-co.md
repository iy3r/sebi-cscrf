title: Incident Recovery Communication
goal: RECOVER
function: RECOVER
version: "1.0"

objective: >
  Restoration activities are coordinated with internal and external stakeholders.
standards:
  - id: RC.CO.S1
    text: >
      Public relations management as defined in the recovery plan shall be
      undertaken in the event of a cybersecurity incident.
  - id: RC.CO.S2
    text: >
      REs shall communicate recovery activities to internal and external
      stakeholders as well as executive and management teams.
  - id: RC.CO.S3
    text: >
      REs shall inform actions taken during recovery process to all related
      stakeholders.
---

## Guidelines

### RC.CO.G1
- **standards:** [RC.CO.S1, RC.CO.S2, RC.CO.S3]
- **applicability:** [mii, qualified, mid-size, small-size, self-certification]
- **mandatory:** false

1. Recovery plans shall be discussed with *IT Committee for REs* by the REs. Such plans shall include stakeholders' coordination in recovery process, and both internal and external communication.
