## Guidelines

### RC.IM.G1
- **standards:** [RC.IM.S1]
- **applicability:** [mii, qualified, mid-size, small-size, self-certification]
- **mandatory:** false

1. While ensuring protection of data, and security of processes, RE's BCP-DR capabilities shall support its cyber resilience objectives, and rapid recovery and resumption of critical operations after cybersecurity incident.
2. REs shall try to incorporate lessons learned from incidents reported (if any) by other REs.

### RC.IM.G2
- **standards:** [RC.IM.S2]
- **applicability:** [mii, qualified, mid-size, small-size, self-certification]
- **mandatory:** true

1. RE's RTO shall be met for all interconnected systems and networks through capacity upgradations and periodic coordinated resilience testing.
2. Recovery plan shall be improved after analysing the learnings from periodic drills.
